Operating Principles

Safety before polish

Synthetic by default

Public demos use generated or permission-safe data, never real student records or private institutional materials.

Teacher-controlled workflows

AI assists with structured drafts and artifacts; human review remains the control point for student-facing work.

Source-grounded reporting

Analytical outputs should trace back to declared source material, reproducible processing, and clear privacy boundaries.
